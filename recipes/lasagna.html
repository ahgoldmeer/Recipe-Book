Lasagna Recipe

This is a recipe for lasagna.

Ingredients:

  • Meat
  • Onion and Garlic
  • Tomato Products
  • Sugar
  • Sugar and Seasonings
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Cheeses (Mozzeralla and Parmesan)
  • Egg
Steps:
  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna in the following order
    1. Meat Sauce
    2. Noodles
    3. Ricotta Mixture
    4. Mozzeralle Slices
    5. Meat Sauce
    6. Parmesan Cheese
    7. Repeat layers, then top with remaining Parmesan
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving
