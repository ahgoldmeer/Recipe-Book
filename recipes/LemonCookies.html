Lemon Cookies Recipe

This is a recipe for Lemon Crackle Cookies

Ingredients:

  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/3 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 package lemon cake mix
  • 1 lemons worth of zest
  • 1/2 cup powdered sugar
Directions:
  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees
  2. Prepare 1 - 2 baking sheets, with parchment paper on them
  3. Pour powdered sugar into a small - medium sized plate or dish
  4. Mix eggs, vegetable oil, cake mix, and zest in a bowl until a uniform dough appears
  5. Using a mini ice cream scoop, or small spoon (anything thats approx. 2 tbs) spoon out dough portions
  6. For each dough ball, roll it evenly in powdered sugar
  7. Place each dough ball on the baking sheet, evenly spaced
  8. Bake for 7 - 9 minutes
  9. Allow cookies to cool shortly on the baking sheet, before transfering to cooling rack
