Lighter Honey Sesame Chicken

Ingredients

Chicken

  • 1 1/2 lbs boneless skinless chicken breasts, cut into 1-inch pieces
  • 2 egg whites
  • 1/4 cup cornstarch
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 3 tablespoons vegetable oil

Sauce

  • 1/2 cup low-sodium chicken broth (divided)
  • 1/3 cup honey
  • 2 1/2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon minced garlic
  • 1 tablespoon minced fresh ginger
  • 1 teaspoon rice vinegar
  • 2 1/2 teaspoons sesame oil
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch

For Serving

  • 1 tablespoon sesame seeds
  • 3 green onions, sliced
  • Cooked rice (optional)

Instructions

  1. Season the chicken pieces with salt and pepper.
  2. Mix chicken with egg whites until coated.
  3. Add cornstarch and toss evenly.
  4. Heat oil in a pan over medium-high heat.
  5. Cook chicken in batches until golden and cooked through.
  6. Whisk together all sauce ingredients.
  7. Cook sauce in pan until thickened.
  8. Return chicken and coat with sauce.
  9. Garnish with sesame seeds and green onions.
  10. Serve immediately.
Tip: Avoid overcrowding the pan for crispier chicken.
